Lasagna

This recipe is for an easy way to make a delicious lasagna. Below you will find the ingredients necessary the way how to make this delicious Lasanyer.

Ingredients:

  • Tomato
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Pasta
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Put a pot with olive oil on the fire over medium heat
  2. Chop the onion, garlic and the tomatoes and put them in the pot
  3. Let simmer until golden brown
  4. Add the ground beef
  5. When everything is browned, put the heat to low
  6. Put on a pot of water and turn the oven on on 180 C
  7. Cook the pasta leaves 2 min
  8. Add layers of sauce, pasta leaves and cheese
  9. Finish with a topping of cheese
  10. Put in the oven for 45 min
