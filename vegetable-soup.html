Vegetable Soup

Description: This recipe is for an easy way to make a delicious soup. Below you will find the ingredients necessary the way how to make this soup.

Ingredients:

  • Tomato
  • Broccoli
  • Leek
  • Carrot
  • Water
  • Stock Powder

Steps:

  1. Put a pot with olive oil on the fire over medium heat
  2. Chop all the vegetables in chunks
  3. Let simmer until golden brown
  4. Add 2 spoons of stock powder
  5. When everything is browned, add 2 liters of water
  6. Let simmer until done
  7. Enjoy!
